Our PCV Structure

Our permanent capital vehicle and operating know-how aligns with the long-term needs of African companiesy

Creating long-term value in Africa?

Our PCV structure is a well-executed permanent capital vehicle strategy which combines the best of strategic and financial Investing

Strategic Investor Mindset

  • Focus on specific market opportunity - industry/industry vertical which allows benefit of deep expertise vs. generalist fund approach
  • Long term investment orientation rather than decisions that seek to boost exit valuation and ensures value maximisation
  • Strong focus on value creation vs. growth for growth’s sake
  • Commitment to identifying and retaining world-class talent

Financial Investor Mindset

  • Highest levels of corporate governance
  • Proper alignment of management and shareholders’ interests
  • Strong focus on value creation vs. growth for growth’s sake
  • Effective financial discipline, e.g. Optimizing balance sheet, capital allocation, investment evaluation

Our Vision

Our investment companies will be guided by a select pool of principles critical to value creation. This includes being customer focused, partner inclusive, talent driven, and ESG rooted.

Our Investment Philosophy

We will achieve value creation goals by deploying our tried and tested toolkit built around 4 levers, including transforming the top-line, improving critical processes, instituting global standards of corporate governance, and Optimising the organization

Our People

Vios is a multi-disciplinary team with decades of investing, and operating experience in Africa, US, and Europe. Our track record reflects deep experience in operating and investing in some of Africa’s largest consumer-driven businesses
