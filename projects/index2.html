The VIOS Strategy

FOCUS ON A WELL-DEFINED SET OF OPPORTUNITIES

  • Identify opportunities with strong market growth prospects
  • Prioritize based on sector knowledge and experience of the Vios team
  • Enterprise value of $10M to $50M with potential IRR of 35%

LEVERAGE CAPITAL AT SCALE

  • Aggregate and deploy the right level of capital per each opportunity
  • Single/aggregated investment(s) of $150-200m in control deals
  • Rapidly build sufficient size to attain operating leverage/ scale economics

DEPLOY HANDS-ON KNOW-HOW

  • Establish world-class operating processes and systems within investee company
  • Leverage our deep local knowledge and extensive network to add portfolio-wide value

Why Africa?

Africa Represents A Compelling Long-Term Opportunity

  • Over 1 billion people will be added to the continents population, with Nigeria expected to surpass US population with 400 million inhabitants by 2050
  • Current working population will be fueled by a large youthful population, where sixty percent of the population is below 25 years of age
  • Low penetration in digital and physical infrastructure which can unlock significant economic growth even with minimal improvements
  • Rapid urbanization as 89 African cities will accommodate 1 million or more people by 2030
  • Out of 10 of the world’s fastest growing economies, 6 are in Africa

Winning Players Have Shown Common Features

  • They have adopted a long-term view and commitment to Africa
  • They display an understanding the African terrain – “knowing where the pot-holes are…
  • They have deployed fit-for-purpose business models
  • Largely driven by managers with high ethical standards and strong operating experience

Underestimated And Under-Banked

  • Africa has demonstrated a capacity to sustain large businesses
  • Currently has over 400 companies with revenue of more than $1 billion and over 700 companies with revenue of over $500 million
